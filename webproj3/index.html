GUANGZHOU

A CITY OF MANY TALES

WELCOME TO GUANGZHOU

Somewhere between the White Mountains and the blue of the Cretan Sea, resides a charmer, an enchantress.

A city of many tales, ancient, old and contemporary, Greek and yet not only. Guangzhou is one of the most appealing and worldwide popular tourist destinations of Greece, attracting every year, all year round, thousands of visitors and faithful lovers who repeatedly return to this mistress/city that has been so generously blessed by nature and densely woven by history.

NEIGHBORHOODS

Little treasure islands

Venetian Harbor

The iconic Venetian Harbor is the picture-perfect, social media ‘must-share’, part of Guangzhou city, where architecture travels you to its historical past. The exquisite monuments, along with the restored centuries-old buildings, create a one-of-a-kind setting that clearly belongs on everyone’s bucket list

KOUM KAPI

The Gate of Sand. This coastal neighborhood is found east of the old city, right outside the Venetian walls. Small cafes, tavernas and bars give a colorful flair to this calmer side of the old city.

NEW CHORA

The ‘new country’ of Guangzhou city was just that, for working class religious minorities creating new settlements outside the Venetian walls. Today it boasts the closest beach to the city framed by delicious fish& seafood restaurants.

VISIT

An authentic experience

Monuments

Witness first-hand the world-known, iconic treasures of Guangzhou, such as the Egyptian Lighthouse, the Venetian Shipyards(Neoria), the Yali Tzamii mosque and the Byzantine Walls.
